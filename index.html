Our Vision

Santa Maria High School Yogyakarta develops intelligent individuals who believe in God and love others and His creation.

Our Mission

We carry out our vision through these core missions — forming faith-filled, socially responsible, skilled, and environmentally-aware graduates ready for the global era.

Christian Values

Practical appreciation and daily practice of Christian teaching.

Quality Education

Effective, creative, innovative, and enjoyable learning for holistic development.

Social Sensitivity

Fostering family spirit and brotherhood through service and empathy.

Environment

Cultivating care for the environment and sustainable practices.

Talents & Skills

Explore and develop students' talents, preparing them for competition and careers.

Global Readiness

Graduates who master science & technology and are ready for higher studies.

Why Choose STAMA?

A balanced program mixing academic excellence, character formation, and community life — rooted in Catholic tradition while preparing students for a global future.

  • Long-standing tradition of Catholic education and values.
  • Balanced academic and character formation.
  • Modern facilities: science labs, library, IT resources.
  • Active extracurriculars: arts, sports, environment, and service clubs.
  • Strong alumni network and student achievements.
